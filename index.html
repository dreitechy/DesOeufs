DES OEUFS MENU

Eggs Benedict

Eggs Benedict is a common American breakfast or brunch dish, consisting of two halves of an English muffin, each topped with Canadian bacon, a poached egg, and hollandaise sauce. It was popularized in New York City.

Scrambled Eggs

Scrambled eggs is a dish made from eggs stirred, whipped or beaten together while being gently heated, typically with salt, butter and sometimes other ingredients.

Shakshouka

Shakshouka is a Maghrebi dish of eggs poached in a sauce of tomatoes, olive oil, peppers, onion and garlic, and commonly spiced with cumin, & paprika.
